Newborn Care

Welcoming a newborn into the family is a joyful and life-changing experience. At Pediatric Health Care Center, our Newborn Care services focus on providing comprehensive support for both babies and parents during this delicate stage of life. We ensure that infants receive the highest level of care to support their growth, development, and overall well-being in the first few months of life.

What We Provide in Newborn Care

  • 🍼 Monitoring feeding patterns – breastfeeding or formula guidance.
  • 📏 Tracking weight, length, and head circumference growth.
  • 🌡 Checking vital signs like temperature, breathing, and heart rate.
  • 👶 Umbilical cord care to prevent infection.
  • 💉 Early vaccinations and screening tests.
  • 😴 Sleep safety education for parents.
  • 🧸 Guidance on newborn hygiene and bathing.
